The Kennerley Lab at the Institute of Neurology, University College London works at the interface of classic economic decision-making and cognitive maps. We apply computational and statistical methods to analyze neural data (spikes, lfp) in NHPs and often collaborate to corroborate and extend these findings in human neuroimaging (fMRI, MEG).

We build open source tools to improve NHP research. We maintain a low-cos, open-source NHP training system Mymou
